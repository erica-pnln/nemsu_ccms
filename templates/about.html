North Eastern Mindanao State University

Complaint and Concern Management System

System Overview

The NEMSU Complaint and Concern Management System (CCMS) is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the process of reporting, tracking, and resolving student concerns and complaints within the university.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, efficient, and transparent channel for students to voice their concerns while enabling administrators to effectively manage and resolve cases in a timely manner.

Key Features

  • 15 Complaint Categories
  • Real-time Tracking
  • Secure Messaging
  • File Upload Support
  • Admin Dashboard
  • Feedback System
